    Meet Jamie

    I grew up in New Jersey my whole life. Currently, I am residing near the Jersey Shore working for the Department of the Navy as a Computer Scientist. I was inspired to jump into UX/UI Design when my mentor did a TedTalk about it. The base that I am working at doesn't have much background or information on UX/UI design which made me want to do it even more. I hope to one day make it my full-time job working in New York City.


    I graduated from Villanova University a semester early in Fall of 2022 with a B.S. in Computer Science also minoring in Cybersecurity. My senior year, I interned at a non-profit called Asian Student Achievement as a Technolgoy Intern.


    Since 2015, every summer I worked as a Lifeguard at my local community pool. In 2021, I was promoted to Head Lifeguard and Swim Lesson Coordinator. I gained majority of my leadership skills during those next 3 years.

    Fun Fact! I played the trumpet for 14 years, from the fourth grade all the way through college. In high school I was Section Leader, Drill Sergent and Drum Major for our marching band. In college, I was in charge of social media and taking pictures.
